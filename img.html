The Google logo is a wordmark featuring the word "Google" written in a sans-serif font called Product Sans. The letters are colored in a playful sequence of blue, red, yellow, blue, green, and red. The logo is known for its simplicity and vibrant color scheme. Recently, the "G" icon, which is based on the wordmark, has been updated with a gradient between the colored segments, reflecting a connection to Google's Gemini chatbot.
